Design a 3D-printable part in less time than it takes to open SolidWorks 🫠

why Conversation?

  • 🐰 it's fast
  • 🐣 it's tiny
  • 😄 it's fun
  • 🤷 it runs on Mac, Linux, and Windows
  • 🙅 it doesn't require an internet connection or any other nonsense

features

direct 2D sketching

no constraint-based sketching; just intuitive draw and transform

direct 3D modeling

no parametric feature tree; just blazing fast undo/redo

completely separate 2D and 3D

no absorbing 2D sketches into 3D geometry
no constraining 2D sketches off of 3D geometry

lowest common denominator filetypes

lines, arcs, and circles for 2D geometry (DXF)
triangle meshes for 3D geometry (STL)


about

Conversation uses the amazing open source Manifold library as its geometry kernel and was inspired by this blog post by Manifold author Emmett Lalish 🚀

Conversation is written by Jim Bern and Nathan Vosburg, with big thanks to our CS345 students for being such great guinea pigs 🐹
